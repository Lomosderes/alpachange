# Alpachange - PRODUCT.md

## Overview
Alpachange is a university-focused platform inspired by Change.org. It allows students to voice their concerns, visualize problems, and follow up on institutional issues within their university.

## Users
- **Students:** The primary users who create petitions, vote, and comment. They seek a voice and tangible changes in their academic environment.
- **Authorities:** University administrators who monitor petitions, provide follow-ups, and resolve reported issues.

## Brand & Tone
- **Institutional & Serious:** Since it's within a university context, the design should feel official and trustworthy.
- **Transparent & Community-driven:** The focus is on visibility and collective action.
- **Accessible & Empowering:** Students should feel that their voice matters and that the platform is easy to use.

## Strategic Principles
1. **Clarity of Progress:** Users should easily see the status of a petition (Pending, In Process, Resolved).
2. **Action-Oriented:** Creating and supporting petitions should be the central, most frictionless actions.
3. **Institutional Trust:** The UI should look like a formal channel for communication, avoiding a "social media noise" feel.
